Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

NoTyDrive is a secure cloud storage app for your files, media, and links. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Google account — email and display name when you sign in with Google.
  • Files & media — content you upload, store, stream, or share through the app.
  • Usage data — basic logs for features, security, and performance.
  • Advertising — Google AdMob may use device or ad identifiers under Google's privacy policy (non‑premium users).

How we use your information

To provide cloud storage, media playback, subtitles, creator earnings, premium subscriptions, account management, and — for free users — relevant ads.

Sharing & third parties

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be processed by Google (Sign‑In, related APIs, AdMob) and our servers to operate the service you request.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. No online service can guarantee 100% security.

Children

NoTyDrive is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Delete your NoTyDrive account

You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time.

How to request account deletion

  1. Email notydrive@gmail.com from the same address used in NoTyDrive.
  2. Subject line: Delete my NoTyDrive account
  3. We will verify your request and delete your account within 30 days.

What is deleted

  • Account profile (name, email, login credentials)
  • Files and media stored on our servers for your account
  • Earnings balance and withdrawal records tied to your account

What may be kept

  • Minimal logs kept up to 90 days for security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance, then deleted.

Delete files without deleting your account

In the app, open My Files and delete individual files or folders you no longer want stored.
